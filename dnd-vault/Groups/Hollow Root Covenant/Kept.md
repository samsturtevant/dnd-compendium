The Kept are members of the [[Groups/Hollow Root Covenant/Hollow Root Covenant|Hollow Root Covenant]] who have had their [Wish](#wish) granted by the [[Buried Star]].

## Wish
A Wish is the manifestation of someone's deepest desire. When members of the [[Groups/Hollow Root Covenant/Hollow Root Covenant|Hollow Root Covenant]] are believed to have fulfilled their highest duty, the [[Buried Star]] grants them their Wish.
