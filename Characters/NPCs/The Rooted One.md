The Rooted One was discovered within the [[Underground Chamber]] beneath the [[Blighted Bog]]. He is an ancient corpse, seated on a throne where gnarled roots emanate outward from his body. His chambers were pilfered by The Party, who ended up stealing [[Gnarled Staff of the Rooted One|his staff]] along with some other magical artifacts.
