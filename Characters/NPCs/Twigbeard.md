# Twigbeard

Twigbeard is a mysterious gnome who resides in the [[Hollow Root Covenant]]. He is known for his love of [[Twigball]] and his impressive beard made of actual twigs.

## Background

Twigbeard joined the covenant many years ago and has become an integral part of the community. His wisdom and unique perspective on nature magic make him a valuable ally.

## Personality

Despite his gruff exterior, Twigbeard has a soft spot for those who show respect for nature. He is fiercely protective of the covenant and its secrets.
