Tobias is a grizzled military veteran with a soft side, who spends as much time sharpening his greatsword as he does pressing flowers. He was part of the original party who lost their memory at the [[Peapod Public House|Peapod Pub]] after unknowingly partaking in some [[Misenchanted Lavender Mead]].
