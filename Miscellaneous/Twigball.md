Twigball is a dice game that pretty much everyone knows about and always wants to play. The goal is to get the biggest numba.

Requires:
- d8
- d6
- d4(s)

The game plays out as follows:
1. Players ante, then roll their d8
2. Players call or raise the bet, then roll their d6
3. Players call or raise the bet, then roll their d4
4. Roll an additional d4 for each pair from the initial d4/d6/d8 rolls
5. The highest total sum is the winner
